Guarantee

We read the mind of our customers and guarantee the international standard of garments. Our garments range meets up with the international consumer require-ments and test stadards of fabric & garments.

Backbone of Industry

Our products are quality tested with various criteria for color fastness, azo-free, shrinkage, flammability, wearing comfort, durability and feel. Our buyers are scattered globally. Technically qualified and efficient work force is the backbone of our industry.

Assured Quality

From sampling to post shipment follow-ups, each step of the production and all the leveling process are being scrutinized and quality is assured by our indefatigable merchandisers, quality controllers and workforce. We manufacture garments for men, women and kids.
