Shape of an Apparel

Fabric goes via diverse procedures earlier than it takes the shape of an garb.

Garment manufacturing isn't always simplest an interest now, it has turn out to be a adventure of artwork. This adventure of fiber to garment starts from selection of fibers which can be then transformed into yarns thru spinning and different strategies. After that begins the adventure from yarn to material which entails tactics like weaving, knitting, tufting, felting and so on. Then comes the completing procedures which prepares the fabric for its beautification via dyeing and printing. All these tactics must be completed with such precision that fabric production has grow to be a complete fledged educational situation with the purpose to produce textile professionals.

Fiber to Garment

Fiber to Garment

Fibers are units of matter having length at least 100 times their diameter or width. Fibers suitable for textile use possess adequate length, fineness, strength, and flexibility for yarn formation and fabric construction and for withstanding the intended use of the completed fabric.

Yarn

Yarn

When the short fibers are twisted together, they take the form of yarn. Thus, yarn is a continuous filament of interconnected fibers used for making textile. Yarn can be made by one of the several techniques of spinning.

Fabric

Fabric

Fabric is formed by networking of natural or synthetic yarn. Weaving and knitting are two of the major methods of fabric construction. Other methods like tufting, stitch-bonding, felting are also used for constructing fabrics. Non woven fabrics have also gained importance these days.

Garment

Garment

The actual ready to wear apparel involves many more processes right from pattern drafting to garment construction which include pattern designing and pattern making, grading, marker making, apparel cutting, sewing, pressing and finishing.
